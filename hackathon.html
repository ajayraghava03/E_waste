About the Project

The E-Waste Challenge is a digital initiative aimed at promoting sustainable electronics disposal. Through awareness, tracking, and participation, schools and colleges can actively contribute to reducing electronic waste, protecting the environment, and creating a cleaner, greener future.

Our platform helps institutions log and manage e-waste collection while encouraging students and staff to understand the importance of responsible recycling. By connecting educational institutions nationwide, we are building a community focused on environmental responsibility.
